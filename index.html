About

Growing up in Baltimore Maryland, Gregory McCoy III otherwise known as Chef Beadie Bead developed his love and passion for food at young age. Since starting in 2020 selling individual gourmet meals, Chef Beadie has built a solid reputation for having the most savory and delicious food you would ever stick a fork in. Chef Beadie is now doing private chef dining and catering small to large events.

"I dont just make food. I make a comfortable and enjoyable experience that you'll never forget!"
